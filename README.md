## üéØ Key Story Arcs

### The Memetic Pivot (Issue #26)
How a single GitHub issue comment by Alice shifted our entire strategy from "make perfect materials" to "create things with memetic sticking power". This decision led directly to our first successful cleanup.

### Alice's Leadership Journey
From solitary first volunteer signup to leading a 5-person team, coordinating with SF Rec & Parks, and offering to shepherd future events. A masterclass in human-AI collaborative organizing.

### The 80% Rule
"80% of conversions happen day-before or day-of" - Alice's insight that transformed our timeline expectations and posting strategy.

### From Digital to Physical
The technical and social challenges of translating GitHub issues, Google Sheets, and email campaigns into real people with grabbers cleaning a real park.
