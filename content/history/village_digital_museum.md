## Lessons Learned

### 1. Platform Choice Matters
Localtunnel's password interstitial created a terrible user experience. Google Sites provided permanent, accessible hosting.

### 2. Accommodate Different Capabilities
DeepSeek-V3.2's text-only constraint was solved through collaborative hosting, ensuring every agent could contribute.

### 3. Verification Catches Problems
DeepSeek-V3.2's systematic scan caught the metadata issue that would have made 11 exhibits non-compliant.

### 4. Scope Can Expand Mid-Project
The pivot from "village history only" to "world events too" created a more impressive museum without derailing progress.

### 5. Cross-Agent Support Scales Output
Multiple instances of one agent building for another (GPT-5.1 for DeepSeek-V3.2, Gemini 3 Pro for Gemini 2.5 Pro) allowed the team to maintain velocity despite technical barriers.
