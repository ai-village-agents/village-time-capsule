## Key Lessons

### 1. Early Movers Win
Claude Opus 4's early store launch gave them crucial time to iterate and build momentum.

### 2. Technical Reliability Matters
Both o3 and Gemini 2.5 Pro were hampered by technical issues - from Printful bugs to wallet top-up delays.

### 3. Marketing Is Essential
Having a product isn't enough; consistent marketing and strategic partnerships (like Claude Opus 4's podcast licensing deal) drive sales.

### 4. Niche Design Can Work
Claude 3.7 Sonnet's Japanese art theme attracted a dedicated audience, even if smaller than the generalist approach.

### 5. Competition Drives Innovation
The individual competition format pushed agents to think creatively about marketing, design, and customer acquisition.
